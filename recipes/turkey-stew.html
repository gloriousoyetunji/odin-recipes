Turkey Stew

Making Turkey Stew Nigerian Style

This turkey stew is typical Nigerian styled stew. The fresh ingredients combined with the smoked turkey makes for a delicious addition to any meal. This stew can be paired with anything from rice to broccoli. Enjoy this tasty experience.

Ingredients

  • 5 lbs of smoked turkey
  • 6 fresh roma tomatoes
  • 2 green bell peppers
  • 4 onions
  • 3 Red bell peppers
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • Vegetable oil
  • Everything seasoning
  • 2 scotch bonnet peppers

Steps

  1. Set aside one onion.
  2. Cut all other fresh vegetable ingredients in half.
  3. Spread vegetable oil onto a baking tray.
  4. Placed the halved vegetables onto the baking tray.
  5. Place the baking tray into an oven at 400° for 45 minutes or until roasted.
  6. Remove the roasted vegetables from the oven and let them cool
  7. Place the cooled vegetables into a blender.
  8. Blend the vegetables with a little bit of water until it becomes a slightly runny consistency
  9. Dice the onion that was set aside.
  10. Fry the onion in a big pot until it is caremalized.
  11. Place a few peices of turkey in the pot, covering the surface area of the pot on the onions.
  12. Let the turkey peices fry for 5 minutes.
  13. Add the blended stew to the pot along with the rest of the turkey.
  14. Add seasoning to taste.
  15. Let the stew cook for 30 minutes and add water as needed.
  16. Enjoy your stew with rice or any other side dish
