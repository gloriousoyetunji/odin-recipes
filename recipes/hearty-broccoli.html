Hearty Broccoli

Making a hearty broccoli snack

This hearty broccoli snack is healthy, tasty and packed with nutrients

Ingredients

  • 1 cup turkey stew
  • Broccoli
  • Water

Steps

  1. Boil or stean the desired amount of broccoli
  2. Heat the turkey stew
  3. Dip the broccoli into the turkey stew
  4. Enjoy
